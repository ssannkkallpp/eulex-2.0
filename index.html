How to Use EULEX

Getting Started

Choose a story from the selection panel. Stories are organized by difficulty level.

Navigation

  • Use arrow buttons to navigate word by word
  • Press Spacebar to move to the next word
  • Click on any word to jump to it

Audio Features

  • Click the speaker icon to hear the current word pronounced
  • Click the play icon to hear the entire sentence
  • Adjust reading speed using the slider

Syllable Display

Each word is automatically split into syllables to help with pronunciation and reading comprehension. Click on individual syllables to hear them pronounced.
